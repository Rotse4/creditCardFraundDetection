The challenge is to recognise fraudulent credit card transactions so that the customers of credit card companies are not charged for items that they did not purchase.


Main challenges involved in credit card fraud detection are:

  • Enormous data is processed every day and the model build must be fast enough to respond to scam in time
  • Imbalanced data i.e most of the transaction(99%) are not fraudulent which makes it really hard for detecting the fraudulent ones
  • data availability as the data is mostly private
  • misclasified data can be another major issue, as not every fraudulent transaction is caught and reported
  • adaptive techniques used against the model by the scammers

How to tackle this challenges?

  • The model used must be simple and fast enough to detect the anomally and classify it as fraudulent transaction as quickly as possible
  • Imbalance can be dealt with by properly using methods
  • for protecting the privacy of the user the dimensionality of the data can be reduced
